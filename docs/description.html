What is Ravine Personal Project?
What does Ravine Personal Project hope to accomplish?
A site that will collect all items available for purchase from all your favorite retailers and present them in one place.
Say you wanted a hat that was from only the brands Nike, Adidas, and Champion. On this site, you would be able to filter by hats, then by only these stores and the site would return items directly being sold from these brands. On other similar sites, they display these branded items as their own being resold. However, this site will just collect information from brand sites and conveniently display them for a shopper.
